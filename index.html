Current travel apps, do not take into consideration that users want to find everything in one place, and some users do not have time to research/plan every piece of their journey.

Research

Accessibility in today's world is something that can be helpful no matter where you are. Imagine planning a trip, business or personal, where you are using different tools to book everything.

Where do you find what you need when you need, and do you have to take screenshots or maybe print it out to keep everything together in one area. Maybe you need to access your airbnb information, because TSA at an international airport is asking you for it, but you forgot to print it out and now you are holding up the line.

People start to get upset, you are frazzled and now the beginning of what should have been a fun trip, has started off very rocky. It would be great to have an app that could easily store and build an itinerary, using the links from your planned trip tools.

Problem Statement

Travelers find it stressful and time consuming having to plan and organize travel. Whether planning in advance or booking a last minute trip, they need a helping hand to achieve that stress free journey.
